Self Care

sunday schedule:

----------morning----------

*9:00am- wake up and play your favorite playlist

* it's a sunday morning so today is focused on resting

*make sure to let a little sunlight into the room, so open any curtains/windows to let in some fresh air

*10:00am- walk over to the kitchen and make yourself some coffee or morning tea with toast(light breakfast)

*it is important to not look at social media before lunch, so grab a book do a little 30 min reading

*journal 3 things you want to achieve day

*clean up for the morning

----------noon----------

*12:00pm- go into your wardrobe and pick out a nice outfit for brunch!

*if the day is nice and sunny, consider taking a bike or walk to your favorite brunch location!

* ENJOY BRUNCH :)

*2:00pm- don't let today's day go to waste, spend some time to soak in the sun

*spend time outside while you rewind from brunch(1hr)

-----------evening------------

*4:00pm- meet someone for dinner: since today is a rest day, lets not worry about cooking tonight!

*bring a small table game/card game for you and your friend to play during dinner while waiting for your food!

*I recommend playing Love Letters

*7:00pm- unwind! take a nice warm lavender bubble bath while playing some soothing music~

*make a lsit on what you need to get done for the week

*10:30pm- time for bed! sleep, put away the electronics and have a nice sleep. Goodnight!

monday schedule:

----------morning----------

*9:00am- it's monday morning! to ensure you are up and ready, take a cold shower to wake up

*monday is a work day, using the list you made on sunday, review over what you need to get done today

*but first lets make some quick breakfast, make yourself some oatlmeal with your favorite topping. I recommend strawberries.

*while munching on some breakfast, look at your email inbox and start clearing them out

*if you're a student, still ensure to have breakfast before starting today's classes

----------noon----------

*12:00pm- take an hour break from your morning work, relax your eyes for a bit and listen to some music

*1:00pm- ensure that you are also inputting time for lunch even if it its for a snack

*set aside time to focus only on work

-----------evening------------

*time to unwind!

*5:00pm- make yourself a nice dinner, chips and soda does not count!

*take your dinner and watch your comforting Netflix series. Not sure what to watch? Here is a list of some top series being streamed in October

*put on some comfortable pj's and make yourself a nice tea

*10:00pm- start heading to bed, after a long day of work it is nice to sleep. Goodnight!

tuesday schedule:

----------morning----------

*9:00am- it's tuesday morning! let's start of with some meditating for 1 hr

*it is nice to slowly wake up your mind with some positive thinking

*10:00am- let's not forget to make some breakfast, a quick eggs and toast with a side of orange juice will be sufficient

*dress up for the day

----------noon----------

*12:00pm- set time to only focus on work, work for 3 hours and then take a break

*3:00pm- break time! let's unwind a little and either go out for an 1 hr workout or take a run around the neighborhood

*4:00pm- once you are back from your active activity, make sure to shower so you can feel refreshed

----------evening----------

*6:00pm- either make yourself some dinner or even order if today's activities where too much. There are always door dash coupons for discounts

*after some dinner, spend some time to do "me" time, I usually enjoy drawing or painting while listening to music

*finish off the day by doing your nightly routine, whether that is skin care or just brushing your teeth

*10:00pm- put on some comfortable pjs and head to sleep, goodnight!

wednesday schedule:

----------morning----------

*9:00am- it's wednesday morning! let's get out of bed and do a morning skin care routine! wash your face and put on a facial mask

*10:00am- breakfast time!- pour yourself a bowl of your favorite cereal

* while eating cereal, read a book or play your comforting morning playlist to get your brain running for the day

*journal what you need to get done for today!

----------noon----------

*12:00pm- set time aside to focus on any work or chores that need to be done. (3hours)

*1:00pm- ensure that you are also inputting time for lunch even if its for a snack

*3:00pm- lets take a break, stretch out your legs and go out for a nice walk outside

* it is important to stay active so either call a friend to go out on a walk or even a light workout

----------evening----------

*make yourself a nice dinner, ensure that you have a healthy plate of protein and veggies

*journal for 10 mins and reflect about how your day went and what you are looking forward to

*8:00pm- either unwind with some video games or even check in with friends

*do your nighly routine, brush your teeth, put on some comfy pjs, and start heading to bed

*11:00pm- bedtime! goodnight!
